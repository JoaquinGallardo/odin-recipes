Chilean Tomato Salad

This is a very simple salad, but thanks to its wonderful ingredientes, it should turn out to be very tasty.

You should start your 3 course meal with this!!!

Ingredients

  • 4 Tomatoes
  • 2 Garlic coves (Opcional)
  • 2 Onions
  • Coriander
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • 2 Green Chilean chilis

Preparation

  1. On a cutting board, cut the onions into thin slices and put them in a bowl filled with hot water and salt.
    You should use your bare hands to squeeze the slices for 7 minutes, let it rest for 10 minutes and then strain them in a colander until the water is removed.
  2. Chope the Garlic and then put them into the serving bowl.
  3. Cut the tomatoes into wedges and put them into the serving bowl.
  4. Cut the chilis into twers and hen, you guessed it, put them into the serving bowl alongside the muffled onions.
  5. Chope the Coriander and add to your salad as well.
  6. Finally, add some salt, black pepper and olive oil at your own discretion and slowly stir your salad.

And that's pretty much it, enjoy your simple, yet delicious salad!!!
