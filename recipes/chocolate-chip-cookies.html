Chocolate Chip Cookies

Getting tired of wasting your money in cookies that don't even taste good?
Or do you wanna impress your someone special with something unexpensive yet so tsty and magical at the same time?
Then you should try these recipe of this tasty crunchy yet soft cookies and I promise you that you'll never regret it.

GIVE IT A TRY!!!

Ingredients

  • Plain flour
  • 2 Eggs
  • Maocene
  • Sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • 500 grs of chocolate chips (Hershey's brand)
  • Vanilla Escence
  • 200 grs of butter
  • Baking Paper

Preparation

  1. In a bowl, add the 2 eggs, 3/4 cup of sugar and beat it until smooth.
  2. Then add 2 teaspoon of vanilla escence, 3/4 cup of brown sugar and melted butter.
    BEAT IT
  3. In another bowl, add 2 cups of flour, half a cup of maocene and 3/4 of teaspoon of baking powder.
    STIR MIX
  4. In a bigger bowl, add the 2 mixes together alongside the whole bag of chocolate chips.
    STIR MIX UNTIL FIRM
  5. Let it rest for 15 minutes
  6. Cover the oven plate with the unwrapped paper and start putting the mix into little balls.
  7. Bake it at 180Â°C for 15 aprox.
  8. Once the cookies are cooked, you must first put them with the baking paper on the fridge for 5 minutes.
    After completing that time, now you can remove those cookis individually
  9. Put them in jar or a dish
  10. ENJOY!!!
