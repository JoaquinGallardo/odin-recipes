Bolognesa Spaghetti Pasta

When it comes to make something simple yet special, most of the times this recipe never fails.
Must be something about the combination of the meat and tomatoes that gives you that flavor explosion in your mouth.
I suggest you all to try this recipe and as a bonus, peoplpe will think you are a chef.
Also for the record, the important part of this dish is the souce, so if you wanna use it with another type of pasta, GO FOR IT!!!

REMEMBER TO COOK WITH LOVE

Ingredientes

  • Olive oil
  • 5 chopped garlic cloves
  • Half choped onion
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • 400 grs. of ground beef
  • Italian Seasoning (Great Value brand)
  • 600 grs of tomato sauce
  • 200 grs of spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Preparation

  1. In a deep frying pan, add olive oil until the surface is covered by a thin layer, the add all together the chopped garlic and the half onion.
    Add salt and pepper at your own discretion, then stir until it gets beautifully golden.
  2. Deposit the ground beef on the frying pan and again, add salt and pepper with the adittion of the Italian Seasoning.
    Stir until the beef is cooked
  3. After the beef is cooked, add the 600 grs of Tomato Sauce with some more of that Italian Seasoning and slowly stir for 5 minutes
    Let it rest at minimum heat while we get the spaghett ready
    4. REMEMBER TO STIR FROM TIME TO TIME WHILE YOU COOK YOUR PASTA
  4. In the meantime, add boiling water and your spaghetti into your cooking pot and let it cooked for 10 minutes.
    OH, AND DON'T FORGET TO ADD SALT!!!
  5. After your Spaghetti is cooked, strain them in a colander until the water is removed.
  6. Finally, served the spaghetti with that delicious sauce on top and don't forget to puor alot of Parmesan cheese
  7. ENJOY!!!
